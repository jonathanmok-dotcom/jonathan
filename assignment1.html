Titanic

Titanic is a 1997 epic romance and disaster film directed by James Cameron. The story follows Jack and Rose, two young lovers from different social backgrounds who meet aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic. As the ship tragically sinks, their love story unfolds against the backdrop of one of history's most infamous maritime disasters.

I love Titanic for its breathtaking visuals, emotional storytelling, and unforgettable performances by Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet. The chemistry between Jack and Rose makes the romance feel genuine, and the historical setting adds depth to the narrative. My favorite character is Jack, whose adventurous spirit and kindness shine throughout the film.

  • Jack Dawson – A talented artist who wins a ticket aboard Titanic in a poker game.
  • Rose DeWitt Bukater – A young woman engaged to Cal, struggling with her privileged but restrictive life.
  • Cal Hockley – Rose's wealthy fiancé, known for his controlling and jealous nature.
  • Molly Brown – A kind-hearted, wealthy passenger, later known as "The Unsinkable Molly Brown."
