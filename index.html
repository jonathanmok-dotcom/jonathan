Titanic (1997)

Titanic is an epic romance and disaster film directed by James Cameron. Released in 1997, it stars Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet as Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social classes who fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

The movie is renowned for its stunning visuals, emotional storytelling, and historical detail. It won numerous awards, including 11 Academy Awards, and remains one of the highest-grossing films of all time.

About the RMS Titanic

The RMS Titanic was a British passenger liner operated by the White Star Line. It was the largest ship afloat at the time of her maiden voyage from Southampton to New York City in April 1912. Tragically, the Titanic struck an iceberg on the night of April 14, 1912, and sank in the early hours of April 15, resulting in the loss of more than 1,500 lives.

  • Length: 882 feet 9 inches (269.1 m)
  • Passengers and crew: Over 2,200
  • Lifeboats: 20 (insufficient for all on board)
  • Maiden voyage: April 10, 1912
  • Sank: April 15, 1912

Main Characters in the Movie

  • Jack Dawson (Leonardo DiCaprio) – A penniless artist who wins a ticket aboard Titanic.
  • Rose DeWitt Bukater (Kate Winslet) – A young woman from an aristocratic family, engaged to Cal.
  • Caledon 'Cal' Hockley (Billy Zane) – Rose's wealthy and controlling fiancé.
  • Molly Brown (Kathy Bates) – A kind-hearted, wealthy passenger known as "The Unsinkable Molly Brown."
  • Ruth DeWitt Bukater (Frances Fisher) – Rose's mother, focused on maintaining their family's status.
  • Captain Edward John Smith (Bernard Hill) – The Titanic's experienced captain.
  • Spicer Lovejoy (David Warner) – Cal's loyal bodyguard.
  • Thomas Andrews (Victor Garber) – The ship's builder and chief designer.
  • Brock Lovett (Bill Paxton) – A modern-day treasure hunter searching for the Heart of the Ocean.
  • Lewis Bodine (Lewis Abernathy) – Brock's friend and team member.
  • Fabrizio De Rossi (Danny Nucci) – Jack's Italian friend and fellow passenger.
  • Harold Bride (Craig Kelly) – Junior wireless operator on Titanic.
  • J. Bruce Ismay (Jonathan Hyde) – Chairman of the White Star Line.
  • Colonel Archibald Gracie (Bernard Fox) – First-class passenger and survivor.
