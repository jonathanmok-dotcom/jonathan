Titanic (1997)

Titanic is an epic romance and disaster film directed by James Cameron. Released in 1997, it stars Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet as Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social classes who fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic. The film is renowned for its stunning visuals, emotional storytelling, and historical detail.
