The Movies - Titanic Profiles

Titanic (1997)

Release Year: 1997
Director: James Cameron
Production Company: 20th Century Fox, Paramount Pictures, Lightstorm Entertainment

Titanic is a romantic drama set against the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic. The film follows Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social classes who fall in love aboard the ship. Their romance is tested by the tragic sinking of the Titanic after it strikes an iceberg.
Source: Wikipedia

Titanic (1953)

Release Year: 1953
Director: Jean Negulesco
Production Company: 20th Century Fox

This earlier adaptation of the Titanic disaster focuses on the lives and relationships of several passengers as the ship makes its doomed voyage. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and survival as the tragedy unfolds.
Source: Wikipedia
